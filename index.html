What is Dev Empathy Book Club?

The idea is simple. The results just might amaze you.

As software developers, we all learn at some point that technical skills alone aren't sufficient. Success and growth in our careers depends on becoming better at soft skills - more empathetic, more caring, better listeners, stronger team players. Together, we'll draw inspiration from general empathy-related works, as well as books written specifically for the programming business context, in both cases learning lessons about how to be better developers, and better people.

The commitment is ambitious but reasonable: One book every 2 months, with discussion held on the forum. The core panel will video chat about the book monthly to provide contrast for your own reading experience.
